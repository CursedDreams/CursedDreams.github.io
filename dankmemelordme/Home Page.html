Badgers4Sale

What we do

Here at Badgers4Sale, we take pride in the sale of good healthy badgers to those in need of one. We all know that every household needs a badger, and not everyone can get one at their local badger store.

What you help with
When you buy a badger, you donate to the Homeless Badgers in New York © fund. Every badger bought is a badger off the streets. We at Badgers4Sale take good care of your badgers, from the factories in China to the shipping depot in Mexico we take the best care of your badgers.

About us

Our founder Matthew Cunningham created this company on February 15, 1890 to help the badgers around the world find a loving home. From that day on, we've vowed to abolish the number of homeless badgers around the world. This branch of Badgers Inc. has collaborated with the Homeless Badgers in New York © fund to help get those lonely cold badgers off the streets. Every day, a badger is beaten, and killed. We at Badgers4Sale want to stop that, and if you buy a badger today we can change that.
